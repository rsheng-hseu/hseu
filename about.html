About Us


Huasheng plastic products was found in 1997 as a joint venture company, with registered assets USD 2.4 million and total investment over 30 millions. It is located in weifang, Shan Dong province and occupies 30,000 sqms and has 120 sets of machines with minimum production capacity of 12,000 tons/year.

We mainly produce shopping bags, garbage bags, freezer bags, Ice Cube bags and dog bags from different materials like virgin and recycled resins of HDPE, LDPE, MDPE, LLDPE, biodegradable and compostable resin and degradable additives. Besides, we also produce pallet cover, stretch film, gloves, aprons, shoes cover, umbrella bags, table cover, cloth cover, cling film and baking/aluminium paper. We could produce customised bags with maximum 8 colours printing.

We proudly export to our products to most European countries and American countries by establishing long term business partnership worldwide. Our factory reached the International Quality Management System ISO9001:2008 and AEO certificated.
